About Trashify

Your platform for waste management and environmental action

Who We Are

Trashify is a citizen-friendly and volunteer-driven platform dedicated to building cleaner and greener cities. We believe environmental change begins with communities, and our mission is to give people the tools they need to take action where it matters most — in their own neighborhoods.

With the support of AI-powered technology and strong community collaboration, we make waste management smarter, faster, and more rewarding for everyone involved.

What Makes Us Different

  • Community-Led: Every citizen can contribute to making their city cleaner.
  • AI-Enabled: Our smart waste classification system makes reporting quick and efficient.
  • Volunteer Recognition: Citizens and volunteers earn Green Credits, badges, and certificates for their contributions.
  • Transparent & Accountable: Track reports in real-time and celebrate community impact.
  • Collaborative Impact: Connecting users, volunteers, and administrators to work together.

Our Platform Features

  • Report waste instantly with photos and geolocation
  • AI-powered automatic waste type classification
  • Volunteer and worker task management system
  • Progress tracking with before-and-after proof
  • Leaderboards, credits, and digital certificates for contributors
  • Community awareness and gamified rewards
